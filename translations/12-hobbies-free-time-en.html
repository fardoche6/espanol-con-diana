🎯

gusta vs gustan

Infinitive → always gusta. Singular noun → gusta. Plural noun → gustan. The verb agrees with the thing liked, not the person.

📌

Article rule

English: "I like music." Spanish: "Me gusta la música." The definite article is never optional after me gusta + noun.

⚡

Jugar al + sport

a + el contracts to al. You can never write jugar a el fútbol. Think: "play at-the football" — the contraction is mandatory.

🔄

Bounce: ¿Y a ti?

After me gusta answers, bounce with ¿Y a ti? — not ¿Y tú?. The preposition a is required before ti / mí / él.
